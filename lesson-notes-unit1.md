# Unit 1 Lesson Notes: Introduction to the Topic

## Learning Objectives
- Students will be able to define key vocabulary for the unit.
- Students will be able to explain the central concept in their own words.
- Students will connect the concept to a real-world example.

## Lesson Outline (50 minutes)
1. **Warm-up (5 min):** Quick-write — what do you already know about this topic?
2. **Direct instruction (15 min):** Introduce vocabulary and core concept with slides.
3. **Guided practice (15 min):** Work through examples as a class.
4. **Independent practice (10 min):** Students complete Part A and B of the worksheet.
5. **Wrap-up (5 min):** Exit ticket — one thing learned, one question remaining.

## Materials Needed
- Slides deck (Unit 1 Introduction)
- Worksheet copies (one per student)
- Exit ticket slips
