# Unit 1 Homework Assignment

**Due:** Next class meeting

## Instructions
Complete the following tasks based on this week's lesson:

1. Re-read your lesson notes and the worksheet from class.
2. Write a short paragraph (5–7 sentences) explaining the main concept in your own words, as if you were teaching it to a friend.
3. Find one example from outside of class (a news article, video, or everyday situation) that connects to what we learned, and describe the connection in 2–3 sentences.
